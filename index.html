AWS

AWS stands for Amazon Web Services. It is the world's most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud computing platform. Instead of buying and maintaining physical servers in a local office or data center, businesses and individuals can rent computing power, storage, and databases over the internet on a pay-as-you-go basis.
